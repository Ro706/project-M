Expertise:

  • Researcher @VNIT, focusing on cutting-edge developments in AI and ML.
  • Proficient in programming languages and adept at designing efficient algorithms.
  • Strong understanding of data structures, algorithms, and database management.
  • Represented my region at the International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC), showcasing strategic thinking and problem-solving abilities

Passion:

I am deeply passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems and drive innovation. My research interests revolve around generative AI and its potential applications in creating intelligent systems.

Mission:
